Vision

To develop an ecosystem where agricultural communities can achieve and lead a prosperous and self-sustainable life with dignity.

Mission

To work with weaker, marginalized, and agricultural communities—especially women, indigenous peoples, family farmers, pastoralists, and fishers—through inclusive agriculture-based knowledge, rural development, financial services, entrepreneurship, research, training, and innovation to drive positive change and advocate sustainable development worldwide.

Philosophy

KGVS believes development must be a "people's programme." Success comes when beneficiaries actively participate. We pool resources and expertise of both governmental and non-governmental agencies to best serve communities.

What We Focus On

Sustainable Farming

Training on improved agronomy, water stewardship, FPO linkages, and climate‑smart practices for better productivity and resilience.

Women Empowerment & SHGs

Formation and nurturing of SHGs, micro‑credit, livelihood activities, and leadership development to strengthen local institutions.

Skill Development & Jobs

Market‑aligned skilling for rural youth—digital, agro‑processing, tailoring, and green jobs—leading to employment and entrepreneurship.

Organic & Natural Farming

Promotion of bio‑inputs, composting, and residue‑free cultivation to lower costs and improve soil biodiversity.

Livestock & Allied Activities

Breed improvement, animal health camps, fodder management, and value‑addition in dairy, poultry and fisheries.

Community Development

Village institutions, digital inclusion, WASH, and participatory planning for inclusive and long‑term rural development.
